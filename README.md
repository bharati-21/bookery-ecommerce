<!-- FEATURES -->

## **FEATURES**

Features include-
- List of categories
- Product listing page
- Single product page
- Filter, search, sort products
- Users can click on a product to view detailed information on the product
- Wishlist Management
- Cart Management
- Address Management
- Orders Management
